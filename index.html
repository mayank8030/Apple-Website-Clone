I — Design

An enclosure
worth the weight.

iPhone 17 Pro and 17 Pro Max begin as a single billet of aerospace-grade aluminium, heat-forged rather than assembled — a construction method borrowed from instrument makers, not phone manufacturers. The result carries more battery, sheds heat more gracefully, and wears its age well.

III — Performance

Power that
holds its shape.

Beneath the unibody sits a laser-welded vapour chamber — Apple's own thermal design, working in concert with the aluminium shell to draw heat away from the A19 Pro chip. The outcome isn't a burst of speed that fades, but sustained performance that holds its line under real, prolonged work.

01

A19 Pro chip

The most capable silicon Apple has fitted to an iPhone — built for demanding work and uninterrupted play alike.

02

Graphics and speed

Paired with the new thermal architecture, CPU and GPU sustain up to 40% more performance over time, not just in bursts.

03

Neural accelerators

Dedicated accelerators inside every GPU core make on-device intelligence faster and more capable than any iPhone before it.

outlast the day.
